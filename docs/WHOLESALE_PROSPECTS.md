## Outreach Cadence

### Email Sequence
**Email 1: Introduction** (Day 1)
- Subject: "Handmade in Litchfield County - SOCIALE Fragrances"
- Introduce brand, founder story, product
- Include line sheet attachment
- Soft close: "Worth a conversation?"

**Email 2: Follow-up** (Day 5)
- Subject: "Re: [previous subject]"
- Share social proof, press mentions
- Offer to send samples

**Email 3: Value Add** (Day 10)
- Subject: "Ma collection - perfect for Mother's Day"
- Seasonal angle
- Limited time opening order incentive

**Email 4: Break-up** (Day 20)
- Subject: "Should I close your file?"
- Direct ask for decision
- Leave door open for future

## Status Legend
- 🔍 Research - Need to find contact info
- 📧 Contacted - Email sent, awaiting response
- 📞 Responded - Store replied, in conversation
- ✅ Approved - Wholesale account approved
- 📦 Ordered - Placed first order
- ❌ Declined - Not interested
- 🚫 Unqualified - Doesn't meet criteria
